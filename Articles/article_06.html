THE BEAUTY OF ARCHITECTURE

In every era and civilization, architecture has always been the most telling measure of a society’s level of development.

It not only responds to practical functional needs but also embodies the spirit and cognitive boundaries of its time.

Throughout humanity’s journey in the vast expanse of cosmic time, monumental architecture evolves.

This is the beauty of architecture.

This is a visual design work exploring the beauty of architecture.

Memory becomes the origin of form.

As a child, I saw ancient architecture and real construction.

As a teenager, I experienced modern construction sounds.

During travels, I discovered diverse architectural beauty.

The work adopts blue as its tonal base.

Geometric forms evolve from primitive to ordered.

Architecture becomes a language of understanding.

Forms transform into modern skyscrapers.

From chaos → order · From nature → reason · From classical → modern

Architecture is the trace of human will.
